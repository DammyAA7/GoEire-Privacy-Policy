GoEire Privacy Policy Last updated: April 2026 GoEire ("the App") is developed by Oluwadamilola Adebayo. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used. Location Data The App requests access to your device's location to display nearby transit stops and enable compass navigation. Location data is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties. Transit Data The App fetches real-time bus and Luas departure information from public transport data sources via our backend service (Supabase). These requests do not contain personally identifiable information. Feedback & Reports If you submit feedback or a bug report through the App, the text you provide is sent to our backend. No personal information is attached unless you choose to include it. Analytics The App does not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or advertising data. Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties. Children's Privacy The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Changes We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
